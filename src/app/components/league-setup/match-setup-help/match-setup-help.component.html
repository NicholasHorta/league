Welcome to League

League is an exciting football simulator for lovers of all things football, either to just pass the time or engage with friends and make the call... will your team lift the cup and retain bragging rights?

The team sheets will auto generate with the finest players the world has to offer along with a list of substitutes. These can be regenerated simply by selecting the Regenerate option which will provide another random selection of teams and players.

Along with this there is the option to go Classic mode. This unlocks a new set of legendary players available in place of the current teams and players.
