League is an exciting football simulator which can be used for personal or group activities when in a competitive or friendly betting mood. A fun way for lovers of all things football to pass the time and get one over each other by succeeding in their call of a team to lift the cup and retain bragging rights over the players.

Just choose which option you'd like to begin with and this will auto generate a team sheet for each team with the finest players the world has to offer along with a list of substitutes. If you would like to regenerate the player lists, simply select the match type option again.

Before being able to progress, each player MUST ensure that a captain is assigned to their team in order to continue on to the Match Day simulations.

  • Quick Match
  • Consists of 2 teams.
  • The simulated game acts as a final.
  • The winner of which is the overall victor.
  • Cup Run:
  • Consists of 4 teams.
  • 4 simulated games.
  • The initial two act as semi finals
  • Runner Up game - Losers of the semi final games.
  • Final game - Winners of the semi final games.
